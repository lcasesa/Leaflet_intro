Most Popular Areas Maintined by the National Parks Service 2013-2021

This map shows visitation statistics across the most popular
areas maintained and protected by the National Parks Service
from 2013 through 2021. As a whole, the National Parks System
hosted an average of more than 264,000,000 total visitors each
year from 2013 to 2021. The most visited sites in 2021 were
Blue Ridge Parkway, Great Smoky Mountains National Park, and
Golden Gate National Recreation Area. Use the slider to change
the year displayed and click on the circles to see specific
park visitation statistics!
